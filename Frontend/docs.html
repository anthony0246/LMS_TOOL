Chatbot API Documentation

Welcome! Here's a quick guide on how to send a message to the chatbot and get a response.

How to Send a Message

To ask the chatbot something, just send a message like, "Hello!" and the chatbot will reply with an answer. If you want to get an analysis of your course content, just upload a CSV version of your course content.

Example

"How can I improve my course contents to better fit the accessibility standards?" (after you have sent the CSV). The chatbot might respond with a potential solution.

What You Need

All you need is a message. The chatbot will take care of the rest and send a reply.
